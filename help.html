Log In & Sign Up Details
How to sign up?
Just click on sign up button to reach sign up page.Then follow the below instruction:
1.Insert your first name.
2.Insert your last name.
3.Choose a Username of your name .
4.Insert your phone number.
5.Insert your email address.
6.Upload your photo .
7.Insert your address there you have to insert your House number, your street name where you from and your city name .
8.Set up a password for your account so that no one can access your account except you .
9.Finally just click on sign up button .

*** if all the documents are okay then you can go for log in .....

How to log in?
Just click on log in button to reach log in page.Then follow the below instruction:

1.Insert your email address what you previously register for.
2.Insert your password what is previously given by you.

*** if all the documents are okay then you can access your account , cart item ,order and much more .....
